#  Heart Attack Risk Analysis & Prediction in India

##  Project Overview

Heart disease remains one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide. This project focuses on analyzing health, lifestyle, and demographic factors that contribute to heart attack risk among patients in India.

Using SQL, Python, Machine Learning, and Power BI, the project identifies key risk factors and develops predictive insights that can support early intervention and preventive healthcare strategies.

---

##  Project Objectives

- Analyze patient demographics and health conditions.
- Identify major factors influencing heart attack risk.
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover patterns.
- Build machine learning models for risk prediction.
- Create an interactive Power BI dashboard for decision-making.
- Generate actionable healthcare recommendations.

##  Key Findings

### Health Factors

- Patients with hypertension show significantly higher risk.
- Smoking is strongly associated with increased heart attack risk.
- Obesity contributes to elevated cardiovascular risk.
- Family history plays an important role in heart disease occurrence.

### Lifestyle Factors

- Alcohol consumption impacts heart attack risk levels.
- Poor lifestyle habits increase overall cardiovascular vulnerability.

### Demographic Insights

- Risk patterns vary across age groups.
- Gender-based differences are visible in risk distribution.
- State-level variations indicate geographical differences in patient profiles.
